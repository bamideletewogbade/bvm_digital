Our Approach

For People's Pension Trust, we developed a comprehensive social media strategy focused on education, trust-building, and accessibility. Our approach included:

  • Creating engaging, visually consistent content that reflects the brand's values
  • Targeting specific demographics with tailored messaging
  • Implementing data-driven posting schedules to maximize reach
  • Developing educational content series to increase financial literacy
  • Leveraging customer testimonials to build trust and credibility
